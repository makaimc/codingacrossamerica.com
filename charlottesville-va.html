Charlottesville, VA

Arrival date: 03.09.2013

Charlottesville is a small city and the home of University of Virginia. There are a mix of small consulting firms servicing the local National Ground Intelligence Center (NGIC) and Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA) defense market, but also some product-based startup companies as well.

Basic stats

  • City population: 43k, Charlottesville + Albemarle County: 120k

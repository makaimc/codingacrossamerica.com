Antengo

Antengo is a location-based classifieds mobile app startup located in San Diego, California.

Antengo logo in their office.

In July 2010, Marcus Wandell (@antceo) and Hunter Jensen launched their mobile app as Anttenna. They rebranded to Antengo a few months later in September 2010.

Technology

Antengo has native apps for both iOS and Android.

The backend services are built in PHP

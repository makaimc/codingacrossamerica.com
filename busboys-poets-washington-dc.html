Busboys and Poets in Washington, D.C.

By Matt Makai on 08.27.2013. Tags: coding spot, coffee

Busboys and Poets is a large coffee shop, bar, and restaurant on the corner of 5th and K Street Northwest in Washington, D.C.

Busboys & Poets sign outside their 5th and K Street NW location.

The 5th and K St Busboys spot is one of two locations they have in downtown Washington, D.C. This place is huge, with two floors and a large outdoor patio. On weekends it can get extremely crowded for brunch. During the week there's a relaxed atmosphere and you can pretty much hang out on your laptop all day without anyone minding.

This is a great spot for getting some coding done. There is fast, open wifi with great coverage. Electric outlets are plentiful under the bar and near some tables - particularly the booths against the back wall.

The music loudness varies. Sometimes I found I could easily listen to my headphones at normal volume while other times I had to turn the sound up. On weekends the place is loud with large crowds talking so it's less comfortable to sit and work for hours at a time.
